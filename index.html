Our Services

Website Creation

Your website is the digital face of your business, and the first impression conveyed to potential customers. However, far too often, sites are designed without consideration of core business goals. A website should capture attention with its beauty and functionality, but also serve as a powerful tool for generating business and marketing your products/services.

At Rubicon, we spend time learning about your business, your potential customers and their needs, your sales strategy, and your goals, before even starting our design process. Our goal is simple: drive and convert traffic.

Today, more than half of all web activity is done on mobile devices (phones and tablets). Our responsive design guidelines ensure that your site looks good on every device.

SEO

A great website is necessary, but what good is it if customers aren’t finding you? 80% of search engine users only visit websites on the first page of results. 29% visit the top result.

Search Engine Optimization is the process of fine-tuning your site so that it is recognized by search engines, most importantly Google, and shown among their top search results. We are on top of industry trends and understand what Google and other search engines require to increase your website’s visibility and drive qualified leads.

Digital Marketing

Want to generate more leads? Pay-per-click (PPC) ad campaigns, most notably Google Ads, allow you to pay for traffic by bidding on keywords relevant to your industry. When these search terms are used, your site is featured at the top of search engine results pages.

In other words, they help you display your ads to potential customers, at the exact moment they're searching for a business like yours. And unlike traditional advertising, with PPC you only pay when someone clicks on your ad.

Let Rubicon manage your PPC campaign.

Social Media Management

Your customers are on social media. 58% of the entire world’s population uses social media. 82% of the population in the United States. On average, people are on social media 2 hours and 27 minutes every day.

At Rubicon, we create content and build engagement between brands and their customers across all major social media platforms. Consistent content and engagement are the most important ways to boost your social presence - building brand awareness, growing your customer base, and ultimately generating more business.
